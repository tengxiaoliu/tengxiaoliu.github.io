About

I'm Tengxiao Liu, a first-year Ph.D. student at University of California, Santa Barbara, advised by Prof. William Wang. I obtained my Master's at Fudan University, advised by Prof. Xipeng Qiu. I was fortunate to work as a research intern at the AWS Shanghai AI Lab, collaborating closely with Prof. Prof. Yue Zhang and Prof. Prof. Zheng Zhang. I received my bachelor degree in Computer Science from Special Gifted Young Program at Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2021.

I am interested in natural language processing and applications of generative language models, with a particular focus on building reliable learning systems that can perform complex reasoning tasks for the common good.
